5. Sources of Information

Information can be gathered from a range of different sources. One of your best sources will be the Pharmaceutical companies that manufacture the original legitimate medications.

However, other sources can generally be divided into 5 categories, these include;

Open Source:
  • TV/Radio
  • Print media
  • Internet
  • Social Media.
Workers and General Public:
  • Fishermen
  • Construction workers
  • Street vendors
  • Sex workers
Service Providers:
  • Freight forwarders.
  • Transport companies.
  • Shipping agents.
  • Doctors.
  • Chemists.
Criminal Sources:
  • Informants
  • Suspects
Official Sources:
  • Officers from your agency
  • Other government agencies
  • International agencies
  • Neighboring Border Liaison Offices
